It’s all about your abundance.

Welcome. I am so glad you are here and curious about how I can support you and your organization.


Organizations are complex and no matter what is going on with you or your team. Together I will help you see the best solutions and strategies for change
by honoring the best of what your team members bring to the table.

Diversity, Equity, Inclusion, and Belonging (DEIB) work is hard and challenging work to do alone. Together I will help you feel supported, prepared, and
confident for the challenges of your work.

I bring over two decades of equity-minded coaching, consulting, facilitation, and teaching experience with individuals and organizations.


Whether you are a professional who works in equity and inclusion or you are an
executive or leader, organizations are complex and the work is hard and challenging
to do alone. I will…


  • Help you feel supported and prepared for the challenges of your work
  • Approach these opportunities from a place of abundance
  • Be a thought partner and a trusted expert for you to turn to
  • Accompany you and your team in your time of growth and development
  • Help you clearly recognize your potential in periods of change

Let’s build your abundance together.
